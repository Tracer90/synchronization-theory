## Статья 1. Основная архитектура

### Целевые журналы

### Abstract (проект, EN)
We present IAC (Invariant Adaptive Core), a self-consistent architecture for adaptive intelligence. Unlike traditional approaches that optimize for external accuracy, IAC optimizes for internal consistency — resolving contradictions between a model's representations, transformation rules, and meta-rules. The architecture is grounded in three layers: Representation, Transformation, and Meta-Transformation, and operates through a Self-Consistent Engine that detects internal conflicts and generates meta-rules to resolve them. We demonstrate that five primitive operations (Distinction, Composition, Tensor, Fixed Point, Adjoint) form an irreducible kernel from which all adaptive behaviours emerge. The system is validated through recursive self-application across 13 research cycles spanning physics, biology, machine learning, and economics.

## Статья 2. Синхронизация и предсказание

### Рабочее название
**"Synchronization as Emergent Property: From Error Minimization to Invariant Discovery"**

### Целевые журналы
- Neural Computation
- Frontiers in Computational Neuroscience
- Entropy (MDPI)

### Abstract (проект, EN)
Synchronization between an agent's internal model and external reality is typically treated as an optimization objective. We challenge this view and propose that synchronization is not a goal but an emergent property of invariant-seeking systems. Drawing on concepts from dynamical systems theory, predictive coding, and evolutionary biology, we show that synchronization arises naturally when a system minimizes prediction error while preserving internal consistency across three levels: state, transition rules, and meta-rules. We define a synchronization metric (sync_error) bounded in [0,1] and demonstrate its relationship to the compressibility of observations. The framework is validated through recursive self-application, cross-domain pattern extraction, and anticipation-verification cycles.

## Статья 3. Научный метод как вычислительный конвейер

### Рабочее название
**"Automating Scientific Discovery: A Falsification-Driven Architecture for Invariant Extraction"**

### Целевые журналы
- Nature Machine Intelligence
- Science Advances (Computational)
- Machine Learning: Science and Technology

### Abstract (проект, EN)
We present the Scientific Discovery Engine (SDE), a falsification-driven pipeline that automates the extraction of architectural invariants from observational data. The pipeline implements the full scientific method: Pattern Extraction → Hypothesis Generation → Contradiction Detection → Falsification → Invariant Extraction → Architecture Integration. Unlike hypothesis-confirming systems, SDE actively attempts to destroy each hypothesis through four contradiction types (logical, empirical, self-referential, cross-domain). Surviving invariants are promoted to architectural primitives and can be integrated as executable code modules. We demonstrate the pipeline through the discovery of a 5-primitive irreducible kernel and its validation across 10 scientific domains.
